# TYPE BUILDER

### The Typing Game That Pumps You Up

---

**Think you can type fast? Prove it on the platform.**

Type Builder is a retro arcade typing game where your keyboard is your barbell. Every keystroke powers your bodybuilder through overhead presses on the gym floor — and when the buzzer sounds, you step on stage to find out if you've got what it takes to beat the competition.

You play as **Tyler**, a rising bodybuilder going rep for rep against three rivals who aren't here to make friends. Type faster. Type cleaner. Take the trophy.

It's the most fun way to get faster at typing — especially if you'd rather pump iron than chase a fox over a lazy dog.

---

## How It Works

You type sentences. Your character lifts.

- Sentences appear on screen — type them as fast and accurately as you can
- Tyler presses the barbell in real time, synced to your typing speed
- The faster and more accurately you type, the more reps you crank out
- When the round ends, your score goes head-to-head against three AI rivals
- Place 1st, 2nd, or 3rd on the podium — or get sent home

That's it. No tutorials. No hand-holding. Just you, a keyboard, and a barbell.

## The Competition

You're not lifting alone. Three bodybuilders are on the gym floor with you, pressing at their own pace:

| Rival | Style | Difficulty |
|-------|-------|------------|
| **"Iron" Ivan Petrov** | The reigning champ. All business, no mercy. | Gold tier (hardest to beat) |
| **Flex McQueen** | The flashy showman. Spray tan. Permanent grin. | Silver tier |
| **Tony "The Tank" Deluca** | The old-school veteran. 30 years in the gym. | Bronze tier |

Beat all three and you take 1st place. Beat none and you're watching from the sidelines.

## Game Modes

**Quick Pump** — One 60-second round. Jump in, compete, see where you land.

**Tournament** — Five rounds of escalating difficulty, from Local Gym Contest to Mr. Type Universe. Place 3rd or better to advance. Get eliminated and you're out.

**Endless Reps** — No timer. Type until you break. How long can you hold it together?

## What You're Typing

Every sentence is pulled from the world of bodybuilding and fitness:

- Gym motivation and lifting quotes
- Real bodybuilding facts and history
- Exercise form instructions
- Nutrition and diet tips
- Trash talk from your rivals

No random pangrams. No boring office memos. Just iron culture, from warm-up to cool-down.

## Scoring

```
Score = (Reps x 100) + Accuracy Bonus + Streak Bonus + Speed Bonus
```

| Component | How It Works |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Reps** | More reps = more points. Type faster to press faster. |
| **Accuracy Bonus** | Above 80% accuracy earns bonus points |
| **Streak Bonus** | Every consecutive correct character builds your streak |
| **Speed Bonus** | Your average WPM, multiplied |

## Speed Tiers

| Your WPM | What Happens |
|----------|-------------|
| 0-20 | Tyler struggles. Sweat drops. It's rough. |
| 21-40 | Smooth, steady pressing |
| 41-60 | Speed lines. Tyler's in the zone. |
| 61-80 | Screen shake. Muscles pumping. The crowd notices. |
| 81+ | Blazing. Afterimage trails. Tyler is a machine. |
